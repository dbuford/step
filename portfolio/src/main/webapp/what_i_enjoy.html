Painting and Drawing

Recently, I have discovered an interest in creative painting and drawing. I find it
incredibly relaxing to just sit down and paint or draw. Specifically during quarantine,
I have started to paint a series titled "Black Women in Color" in which I depict an element
or elements of black women using lots of bright colors. In addition to this, I have also
decided to try my hand in painting shoes. I believe my first pair turned out really well,
and I am excited to try even more

Watching and Studying Television and Film

Over the years, I have watched several films and television series of different genres, my
favorite being comedy, documentary, action, science-fiction, and thriller. However, for me,
I'm not just simply watching for entertainment. Rather, I watch for both entertainment as
well as to study the different elements of the film or series like camera angles, costume,
set design, and, of course, special effects. I hope to one day be a part of the technical
side of the film industry.

Activism

Ever since my first march and protest, I have seen just how import grass-roots activism is.
Starting my freshman year of highschool, I have worked to get involve, both at my school
and in my community to foster a community of change. I have seen first hand racism,
sexism, classism, xenephobia, and more, and I want to do my part to combat these systemic issues.
